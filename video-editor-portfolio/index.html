About

8 years of turning footage into feeling

I'm Alex Rivera, a video editor and creative director based in Los Angeles. I've worked with brands like Nike, Apple, and Spotify, as well as independent filmmakers and musicians.

My approach is story-first. Every cut, every transition, every color grade serves the narrative. I believe the best editing is invisible — it makes you feel without knowing why.

Tools: DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Cinema 4D.
